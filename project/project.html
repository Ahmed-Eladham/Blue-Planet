It is an educational site based on a puzzle game and storytelling that is readable in a special awareness for middle and high school students, whether they are healthy people or people with visual impairments, to increase understanding and education about water pollution and the impact of this pollution on climate change and human health and the appropriate actions that need to be taken. And that through the audio books and storytelling for the students to be within the story about what the hero should do, make them think. Let them think about making the right decision and choosing the right answer and then explain the particular part on this level of the website but in the part of the puzzle game, the student has to find the differences in the picture and then explain to him the reality of these pictures and the extent of the impact of water pollution on the planet and in the case of a large number of points, he wins first place in his region and here we play on the competition part among the students so that awareness is spread and a strong generation is built to support
