What are the steps to develop our project?

Start with pictures of water on our planet Earth and then determine the geographic location of the student living in it so that they can consider the environment affected by water pollution and climate change Make a sequence of steps for pollution problems starting gradually until you reach the solution to the problem to facilitate the delivery of information to the reader or listener The extent of the impact of water pollution and the fundamental role of climate change in altering the course The shape of water Continuously updating the books based on what is happening in the world that directly and indirectly affects water Work on adding appropriate development games with students under the umbrella of gas to visualize the shapes of water, whether polluted or unpolluted
